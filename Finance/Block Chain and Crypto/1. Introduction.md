## ðŸ”¨ 8. Consensus Mechanisms

### Proof of Work (PoW)
- Uses computation and energy
- Extremely secure
- Slow and energy-intensive

**Used by:** Bitcoin

---

### Proof of Stake (PoS)
- Validators stake tokens
- Faster and energy-efficient
- Security depends on economic incentives

**Used by:** Ethereum, Solana, Cardano

## ðŸª™ 10. Coin vs Token
